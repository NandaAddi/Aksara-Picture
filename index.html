The Studio

Aksara Picture is a graduation photography studio based in Malang, present to capture your graduation moments with elegant, natural, and story-filled results. We believe that graduation is not just a ceremony, but an important milestone in life worth remembering with the best visuals.

With experience handling hundreds of graduation photo sessions, Aksara Picture serves both indoor and outdoor shoots, whether personal, with family, friends, or partners. Our photography style adapts to the client's character, ranging from classic formal, soft aesthetic, to warm and memorable candids.

Not only in Malang, we are also ready to roll to Surabaya and Kediri, accompanying every graduate's step without hassle. Supported by professional photographers, quality equipment, and neat natural editing processes, we are committed to providing timeless photo results worthy of sharing on your special day.
